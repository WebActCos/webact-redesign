Local SEO vs National SEO

Local SEO wins nearby customers. National SEO builds broader visibility.

If your customers are nearby, local SEO is critical. If you sell nationally or compete beyond one market, national SEO becomes more important. Many businesses need both.

FAQ

Local SEO questions businesses ask before starting.

What is local SEO?

Local SEO is the process of improving your website, Google Business Profile, listings, reviews, and location signals so nearby customers can find your business in search and map results.

Do I need local SEO if I already have a website?

Yes, if you serve a local market. Your website is important, but local SEO also depends on maps, reviews, listings, local pages, and profile quality.

How long does local SEO take?

Some cleanup and profile improvements can help quickly, but competitive local visibility usually requires consistent work over time.

Can WebAct help with Google Business Profile?

Yes. WebAct can help review categories, services, posts, photos, profile content, reviews, and how the profile connects to your website.

Should I use local SEO or Local Services Ads?

Local SEO supports long-term visibility. Local Services Ads can generate paid lead flow. Many local service businesses benefit from both.
