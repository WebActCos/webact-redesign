SEO vs Paid Advertising

SEO builds long-term visibility. Advertising creates faster traffic.

Most businesses need both at different stages. SEO helps you build lasting visibility and trust. Advertising helps generate traffic while SEO is growing or when you need faster campaign results.

FAQ

Marketing questions businesses ask before starting.

What marketing service should I start with?

It depends on your current website, local visibility, search rankings, budget, and goals. WebAct can review your situation and recommend SEO, local SEO, AI optimization, listings, advertising, or a combination.

How long does SEO take?

SEO is a long-term investment. Some technical and local improvements can help quickly, while competitive rankings and content authority typically take time and consistent work.

Do I need local SEO if I already have a website?

Yes, if you serve a local market. Local SEO helps your business show up for location-based searches, maps, service areas, reviews, and local discovery.

What is AI optimization?

AI optimization helps structure your website content so AI answer tools and search engines can better understand your business, services, locations, expertise, and helpful answers.

Can WebAct manage marketing and advertising together?

Yes. WebAct can connect SEO, local visibility, AI optimization, Google Ads, social ads, landing pages, and website improvements into one growth plan.
