National SEO vs Local SEO

National SEO grows broader visibility. Local SEO wins nearby customers.

The right approach depends on your market. Businesses selling nationwide need broader service and topic authority. Local businesses need map visibility, reviews, location signals, and local landing pages.

National SEO

Best for businesses that want to rank for competitive service, product, industry, and informational keywords across larger markets.

FAQ

National SEO questions businesses ask before starting.

How long does national SEO take?

National SEO is usually a long-term investment. Some improvements can help quickly, but competitive rankings often require months of consistent technical, content, and authority work.

Is SEO still worth it with AI search?

Yes. AI search increases the need for clear, helpful, well-structured website content that explains your services, expertise, answers, and trust signals.

Do I need new content for SEO?

Most businesses need a mix of improved existing pages and new supporting content. WebAct focuses on pages that support rankings and conversions.

Can WebAct do SEO and website updates?

Yes. WebAct can improve the website itself, not just send you a report. That helps SEO recommendations turn into actual implementation.

Should I use SEO or advertising first?

If you need traffic quickly, advertising may help sooner. If you want long-term visibility, SEO is important. Many businesses benefit from both.
