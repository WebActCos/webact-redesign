FAQ

Local listings questions businesses ask before starting.

What are local listings?

Local listings are business profiles across directories, maps, review sites, navigation apps, and search platforms that display your business information.

Do local listings help SEO?

They can support local trust and consistency. Listings work best as part of a local SEO strategy with a strong website and Google Business Profile.

Can WebAct fix duplicate listings?

WebAct can review duplicate listings, recommend cleanup, and help align your business information across important platforms.

How often should listings be updated?

Listings should be reviewed whenever business details change and monitored regularly because incorrect information can reappear over time.
