Frequently asked questions.

Answers for businesses that need professional graphic design without adding another disconnected vendor.

What types of graphic design can WebAct create?

WebAct can create digital ad graphics, social media creative, flyers, brochures, menus, sales sheets, signage, banners, email graphics, presentation visuals, website graphics, and campaign asset packages.

Can WebAct match our current logo and website?

Yes. We can use your current logo, colors, fonts, photos, and website style so new graphics feel like part of the same brand.

Do you create graphics for digital advertising?

Yes. We create ad creative for display, social, search support assets, retargeting, and campaigns that need multiple sizes or message variations.

Can you prepare files for printing?

Yes. We can prepare print-ready PDF files and coordinate standard bleed, trim, and resolution requirements when your printer provides specs.

Can we request ongoing monthly design help?

Yes. WebAct can support ongoing design needs for businesses that regularly need promotions, social content, email graphics, ad creative, and updated sales materials.

Can you redesign old flyers or sales sheets?

Yes. If your materials feel dated or inconsistent, we can rebuild them into a cleaner, more professional format while keeping the core message.

Can you help if we do not have brand guidelines yet?

Yes. We can create a practical design direction using your logo and business goals, then document simple rules for future consistency.

How quickly can a design project start?

Most projects can begin once we have the project details, copy, brand assets, and deadline. Rush timing depends on scope and asset readiness.
