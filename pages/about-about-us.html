Who We Are

A Denver web design and marketing team built for growing businesses.

WebAct brings designers, developers, and marketing experts together around professional websites, modern design, website-builder support, and conversion-driven marketing strategies. The goal is to help each business communicate its voice, brand, and identity in a way that is easier for customers to understand and act on.

Our History

Founded in 2012 with a focus on affordable websites and digital marketing.

Since 2012, WebAct has specialized in affordable websites, design, and digital marketing for growing businesses. Over the years, the team has partnered with technology, marketing, and web companies to develop methods that support stronger web presence, clearer websites, and effective marketing strategies.

How WebAct Helps

Professional websites, practical support, and marketing that can grow with the business.

WebAct works alongside clients through the website and marketing process, creating unique websites that look professional, work well, attract customers, and support campaigns that help businesses grow for years to come.
