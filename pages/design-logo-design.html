Logo Design FAQ

Questions businesses ask before starting a logo project.

What makes a professional logo different from a quick template logo?

A professional logo is designed around your business goals, audience, competitors, and real-world use cases. It is built to scale, reproduce cleanly, and stay consistent across your website, ads, print, and signage.

Can WebAct redesign my current logo?

Yes. We can refresh your existing logo, clean up the artwork, modernize the typography, improve readability, and create proper file formats without losing the recognition your current customers already have.

Do I get files for my website and print materials?

Yes. Final delivery can include transparent PNG files, JPG files, vector files, black and white versions, color versions, and layout variations for web, print, social media, and vendors.

Can you match the logo to my website design?

Yes. WebAct designs logos with your website and marketing in mind, so your logo, colors, buttons, typography, and landing pages feel connected.

How many concepts do I receive?

The number of concepts depends on the project scope. Most projects begin with several visual directions so you can choose the strongest route before refinement.

Can you create business cards, social graphics, or brand materials too?

Yes. Logo projects can be paired with graphic design and branding services for business cards, social profile images, email signatures, flyers, brochures, and ad creative.

Can you help choose colors and fonts?

Yes. We can provide color palette direction and font recommendations so the logo is easy to apply across your website and marketing materials.

How do I get started?

Contact WebAct with your business name, industry, style preferences, current website if you have one, and any examples you like. We will help scope the right logo package.
