FAQ

Branding questions businesses ask before starting.

What is included in branding?

Branding can include positioning, messaging, logo usage, colors, typography, visual direction, graphics, website style, and consistency rules for marketing materials.

Do I need branding or just a logo?

If you only need a mark, logo design may be enough. If your website, messaging, graphics, and marketing feel disconnected, a broader branding project may be better.

Can WebAct apply branding to my website?

Yes. WebAct can connect your brand direction to website design, landing pages, graphics, social assets, and advertising creative.

Can branding help marketing performance?

Yes. Clear branding can improve trust, recognition, message consistency, and the quality of your website and campaign experience.
