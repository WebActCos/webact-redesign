Our graphic design process

A clear process helps protect quality, avoid unnecessary revisions, and keep the project aligned with your goals.

1. Discover

We review your business, audience, goals, brand, competitors, and required uses.

2. Plan

We organize the message, priorities, visual direction, formats, and production needs.

3. Create

We develop initial concepts around clarity, consistency, attention, and action.

4. Refine

We review feedback, improve the selected direction, and prepare it for approval.

5. Deliver

We export the agreed files for web, print, advertising, social media, or production.

Common design deliverables

Digital Files

PNG, JPG, WebP, SVG, PDF, and platform-specific exports.

Print-Ready Files

High-resolution PDFs prepared around bleed, trim, size, color, and resolution requirements.

Scalable Artwork

Campaign Variations
