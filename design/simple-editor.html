FAQ

Simple Website Editor questions businesses ask before starting.

Can I start before paying for hosting?

Yes. You can begin building and customizing before publishing. Your work is saved as you go.

What happens when I click Create Website?

The popup asks for basic business information and then starts the Simple Editor flow.

Can I connect my own domain?

Yes. When you are ready to publish, you can connect an existing domain or register a new one and point it to WebAct servers.

Should I use the simple editor or professional design?

Use the simple editor if you want a fast starter site. Choose professional website design if you need stronger strategy, custom layout, SEO structure, content, and conversion planning.
