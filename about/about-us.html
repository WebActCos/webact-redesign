Our History

Helping businesses build a stronger web presence since 2012.

WebAct was founded in 2012 with a practical goal: help businesses get professional websites and digital marketing support without making the process confusing, overpriced, or disconnected. What started with affordable website design has grown into a broader digital growth company supporting website design, development, SEO, advertising, local visibility, branding, hosting, and ongoing support.

Over the years, WebAct has worked with businesses across restaurants, healthcare, home services, professional services, ecommerce, local companies, and growing organizations that need a stronger online presence. The focus has remained the same: create websites that are easier to use, easier to manage, and better connected to real business growth.

How WebAct Helps

Websites, marketing, advertising, and support working together.

WebAct helps businesses move beyond a basic website by connecting design, content, search visibility, advertising, local listings, and support into one more complete digital presence. That means the website is not just built to look good. It is built to explain services clearly, guide visitors toward action, and support long-term marketing campaigns.

Our team works with clients on the details that matter after launch too: improving pages, updating content, supporting advertising campaigns, strengthening local visibility, and helping businesses keep their websites current as their goals change.
