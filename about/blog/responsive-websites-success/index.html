Whether you're on your computer, tablet, or phone, more and more companies are starting to focus on creating the best visual experience across different multiple screens in order to provide a custom experience among their customers. The best way they can achieve this is by making their websites "responsive". A responsive website is one website that is optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile. With a responsive design you can read, navigate and interact with a website on the device of your choice without having to zoom in or out. This means that a website will adjust it's presentation in order to be easily viewed through different devices.
