Publishing Questions

Frequently asked questions.

Is this a monthly subscription?

Yes. Business Publishing is $100 per month and eCommerce Publishing is $200 per month. Checkout and recurring billing are securely processed through Stripe.

Which plan should I choose?

Choose Business Publishing for a standard informational or service website. Choose eCommerce Publishing when the website includes products, shopping, or online checkout.

Does the plan include a domain name?

Domain registration may be separate. WebAct can help connect an existing domain or assist with domain registration and configuration.

Can WebAct help me publish the website?

Yes. WebAct can help review the website, connect the domain, configure publishing requirements, and prepare the site to go live.
