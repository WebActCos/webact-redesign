Can WebAct set these up for me?

Yes. WebAct can help recommend, configure, and connect add-ons so they work correctly with your website.

Do I need all four add-on types?

Not always. Most businesses start with a domain and professional email, then add widgets or apps as their website needs grow.

Are widgets different from apps?

Widgets are usually website features placed directly on pages. Apps are often larger tools that add expanded functionality such as booking, chat, reviews, or automation.
