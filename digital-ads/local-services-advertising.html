FAQ

Local Services Ads questions businesses ask before starting.

Are Local Services Ads the same as Google Ads?

No. Local Services Ads are focused on verified local service providers and lead generation, while Google Ads provides broader campaign control through keywords, audiences, landing pages, and ad formats.

Does WebAct help with Google Guaranteed?

WebAct can help review the setup path, profile details, service categories, and advertising readiness. Verification requirements are handled through Google's process.

What businesses are best for Local Services Ads?

Home services, roofers, plumbers, HVAC companies, legal services, real estate, cleaning, moving, and other local service businesses often benefit most.

Do I still need a good website?

Yes. Even when leads come through LSAs, people often check your website, reviews, services, and credibility before choosing who to contact.

Can WebAct manage LSAs with Google Ads?

Yes. WebAct can help align Local Services Ads with Google Search Ads, landing pages, SEO, local listings, and your website strategy.
