Local Services Ads FAQ

Questions businesses ask before starting Local Services Ads with WebAct.

How are Local Services Ads different from Google Search Ads?

Local Services Ads are designed for local service businesses and focus on leads such as calls and messages. Google Search Ads are broader pay-per-click campaigns that can promote many types of products, services, and landing pages.

Can WebAct help with Google call ads?

Yes. WebAct can support call-focused Google campaigns, call extensions, mobile-first messaging, and tracking so phone calls are part of the advertising strategy.

Do I need the Google Guaranteed badge?

Eligibility depends on your business category and Google's verification requirements. When available, the badge can help increase trust, but profile quality, reviews, service targeting, and follow-up still matter.

What businesses are a good fit?

Local Services Ads are often a strong fit for home services, legal services, healthcare, auto repair, cleaning, pest control, roofing, HVAC, plumbing, electrical, and other local service providers.

Can I control where leads come from?

Yes. Service areas, categories, budget settings, and profile details help shape where and how your business appears. WebAct reviews those settings so campaigns stay focused on the right opportunities.

Will you review lead quality?

Yes. We look beyond volume and review whether inquiries match your services, locations, and business goals so future optimizations are based on useful lead quality signals.
