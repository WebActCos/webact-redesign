Social Media Ads FAQ

Questions businesses ask before starting social advertising with WebAct.

Which social advertising platform should my business use?

The right platform depends on your audience, offer, budget, creative assets, and goal. Meta is often strong for broad reach and retargeting, TikTok for short-form discovery, LinkedIn for B2B, X for conversation and timely visibility, and YouTube for video education and remarketing.

Do you manage Meta Ads?

Yes. WebAct manages Facebook and Instagram campaigns for lead generation, ecommerce, events, retargeting, local offers, and brand awareness.

Can you manage YouTube Ads?

Yes. WebAct can manage YouTube campaigns for awareness, video views, remarketing, product education, local reach, and supporting longer sales cycles.

Do I need video creative?

Video helps, especially for TikTok and YouTube, but not every campaign needs expensive production. We can help structure campaigns around available images, short videos, graphics, testimonials, offers, and landing page content.

Can social ads generate leads?

Yes. Social ads can generate leads when the audience, offer, creative, form, landing page, and follow-up process are aligned. We focus on lead quality instead of only clicks and impressions.

Will you set up tracking?

Yes. WebAct helps connect pixels, conversion events, lead forms, landing pages, and reporting so campaigns can be optimized around real actions.
