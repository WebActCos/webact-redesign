Social Ads vs Search Ads

Social advertising creates demand while search advertising captures demand.

The best advertising plan often uses both. Social ads help you reach and retarget audiences. Search ads help you capture people actively looking for a solution.

FAQ

Social media advertising questions businesses ask before starting.

Which social platform should my business advertise on?

It depends on your audience, offer, budget, and sales process. WebAct helps choose the right platform mix instead of forcing every business into the same channel.

Do social ads work for local businesses?

Yes. Local businesses can use social ads for awareness, promotions, retargeting, events, lead generation, and keeping their brand visible in the local market.

Do I need landing pages for social ads?

Often yes. Dedicated landing pages can improve message match, conversion rate, tracking, and follow-up quality.

Can WebAct manage social ads with Google Ads?

Yes. WebAct can connect social campaigns with Google Ads, Microsoft Ads, landing pages, SEO, local listings, and website support.
