Amazon Ads vs Google Ads

Amazon captures marketplace shoppers. Google captures wider search demand.

Amazon Advertising is strong when products are already sold on Amazon. Google Ads can support broader demand, product education, ecommerce traffic, and brand-owned website sales.

FAQ

Amazon Advertising questions businesses ask before starting.

What types of Amazon ads can WebAct help with?

WebAct can help with Sponsored Products, Sponsored Brands, Sponsored Display, campaign strategy, product page review, targeting, and optimization planning.

Do Amazon ads work if my product pages are weak?

Ads can send traffic, but product page quality affects whether shoppers buy. Titles, images, reviews, price, offer, and trust signals matter.

Can Amazon Ads and Google Ads work together?

Yes. Amazon Ads can support marketplace sales while Google Ads can help with broader search demand, landing pages, remarketing, and brand-owned traffic.

How much should I spend on Amazon Advertising?

Budget depends on your category, competition, margins, product price, goals, and current sales volume. WebAct can help review the opportunity before scaling.
