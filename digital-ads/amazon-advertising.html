Amazon Ads FAQ

Questions sellers ask before starting Amazon Advertising with WebAct.

What types of Amazon Ads can WebAct manage?

WebAct can help with Sponsored Products, Sponsored Brands, Sponsored Display, keyword targeting, product targeting, search term review, listing improvement guidance, and reporting. For larger brands, we can also help evaluate Amazon DSP opportunities.

Do Amazon Ads work for new products?

They can, but new products may need stronger listing content, competitive pricing, reviews, and launch strategy before scaling spend. We review product readiness before recommending aggressive budgets.

What is ACoS?

ACoS stands for advertising cost of sales. It helps compare ad spend to attributed sales. WebAct uses ACoS, ROAS, conversion rate, revenue, and product-level performance to guide optimization.

Can you help improve product listings?

Yes. We can review titles, bullets, images, descriptions, pricing, reviews, and messaging so campaigns send traffic to listings that have a stronger chance to convert.

Should I run Amazon Ads if I also have a website?

Often, yes. Amazon Ads can capture marketplace shoppers while your website, Google Ads, social ads, and email marketing build a broader ecommerce system.

How do you decide which products to advertise?

We look at margin, demand, competition, reviews, inventory, conversion potential, and business priorities before deciding which products deserve more budget.
