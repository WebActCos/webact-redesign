Microsoft Ads FAQ

Questions businesses ask before starting Microsoft Advertising with WebAct.

Is Microsoft Advertising the same as Bing Ads?

Bing Ads is now part of Microsoft Advertising. The platform includes search advertising on Bing and partner placements, plus shopping, audience, and remarketing capabilities.

Should I run Microsoft Ads if I already run Google Ads?

Often, yes. Microsoft Ads can add incremental search traffic and may reveal different costs, audiences, and opportunities. WebAct reviews whether it fits your budget and goals before scaling spend.

Can campaigns be imported from Google Ads?

Campaigns can often be imported, but they should not be left unchanged. WebAct reviews keywords, match types, budgets, tracking, audiences, and landing pages so the account is optimized for Microsoft Advertising.

Does Microsoft Advertising work for local businesses?

Yes. Local service providers can use search campaigns, location targeting, call-focused messaging, and landing pages to generate calls, forms, and appointment requests.

Can you manage Microsoft Shopping Ads?

Yes. WebAct can help ecommerce businesses with shopping campaign structure, product segmentation, feed review, remarketing, and revenue-focused reporting.

How do you measure performance?

We focus on outcomes such as calls, forms, purchases, quote requests, cost per conversion, lead quality, and revenue signals instead of only clicks and impressions.
