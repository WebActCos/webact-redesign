Why Streaming TV

Television reach with digital targeting and reporting.

Streaming TV advertising combines the impact of full-screen video with audience and geographic controls that are more flexible than many traditional television buys. Audience-focused delivery

Local market precision

Focus reach on selected cities, ZIP codes, service areas, regions, or designated markets rather than paying for viewers outside your target area.

Brand awareness and recall

CTV, OTT, and Traditional TV

Understand the difference before choosing a campaign.

These terms are related but not identical. The right mix depends on where your audience watches, how precisely you need to target, and whether the main goal is broad reach, household-level visibility, local awareness, or measurable digital response.

Connected TV and OTT

Connected TV generally refers to video watched on an internet-connected television. OTT refers to video delivered over the internet rather than through a traditional cable or satellite system.

  • Smart TVs and streaming devices
  • Streaming apps and FAST channels
  • Audience and household targeting where supported
  • Digital reporting and optimization signals

Traditional Television

Traditional television buys can include broadcast, cable, local programming, network schedules, and broader geographic or demographic reach.

  • Scheduled programs and dayparts
  • Broad local or regional visibility
  • Strong reach for mass-market messages
  • Can complement targeted streaming campaigns

FAQ

Streaming television advertising questions.

What is connected TV advertising?

Connected TV advertising places video ads within eligible content watched on internet-connected televisions, including smart TVs and televisions connected through streaming devices.

What is OTT advertising?

OTT advertising refers to video advertising delivered over the internet rather than through a traditional cable or satellite television connection.

Is streaming TV the same as traditional television?

No. Both use television-style video, but streaming TV is delivered through internet-connected apps and devices and can offer different targeting, buying, and reporting options.

Can I target only my city or service area?

Many campaigns can be limited by city, ZIP code, region, designated market, or other geographic criteria, depending on inventory and platform capabilities.

Can I choose a specific streaming platform?

Sometimes. Specific publisher access depends on the buying method, geography, budget, inventory, audience, and platform rules. A media plan should identify whether a platform is guaranteed or only part of a broader inventory pool.

Can my commercial appear on Roku, Hulu, Peacock, or Tubi?

Potentially, when the selected campaign, market, budget, and inventory source provide access. No specific service should be considered guaranteed unless it is listed in the approved media plan.

Do I need a finished television commercial?

You need approved video creative that meets the selected platform's technical and editorial requirements. WebAct can help with messaging, creative direction, production planning, editing, and campaign alignment.

How long should a streaming TV commercial be?

Common lengths include 15 and 30 seconds, but accepted durations vary by platform and inventory. The media plan and creative specifications should be confirmed before final production.

Can I retarget previous website visitors?

Some campaign setups can use privacy-compliant first-party or retargeting audiences, subject to platform policies, consent requirements, data availability, and minimum audience sizes.

How much does streaming TV advertising cost?

Cost depends on campaign management, advertising budget, market size, audience, inventory, targeting, duration, creative production, and minimums. Visit the advertising pricing page or request a custom media recommendation.

What metrics can I see?

Depending on the platform, reporting may include impressions, reach, frequency, video completion, geography, device, audience delivery, website activity, and available attribution signals.

Can streaming TV work with Google Ads?

Yes. Streaming TV can build awareness before a customer searches, while Google Ads can capture active demand after viewers recognize the brand or need the service.

Can small and local businesses use streaming TV?

Yes. Local businesses may use streaming TV to focus on selected markets, reach nearby households, promote locations, and support seasonal or competitive campaigns.

Is placement on a specific show guaranteed?

Not unless the approved media plan specifically guarantees that show or placement. Many campaigns buy audiences or inventory pools rather than a single named program.
