Television Advertising FAQ

Questions businesses ask before starting Streaming TV and CTV campaigns.

What is Connected TV advertising?

Connected TV advertising places video ads on internet-connected televisions and streaming devices. It gives advertisers a way to reach viewers watching streaming content while using targeting and reporting options closer to digital advertising.

Is Streaming TV advertising only for large companies?

No. Modern CTV campaigns can support local, regional, and national budgets. The right budget depends on audience size, geography, campaign length, and reach goals.

Can I advertise only in my local market?

Yes. Campaigns can be built around specific cities, regions, service areas, or broader markets depending on your business goals.

Do I need a finished TV commercial?

You need video creative, but it does not always have to be a traditional broadcast commercial. WebAct can help plan the message, offer, call to action, and supporting landing page.

Can TV ads work with Google Ads and social ads?

Yes. Television builds awareness, while Google Ads, social ads, retargeting, and landing pages help capture interest after people see or remember your brand.

How do you measure results?

We review campaign delivery, reach, completion rates, geography, pacing, website activity, search lift signals, and related conversion paths to evaluate performance.
