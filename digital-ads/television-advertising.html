Streaming TV vs Search Ads

Streaming TV builds awareness. Search ads capture active demand.

Video advertising helps people remember your brand before they search. Search ads help capture people who already know what they need. Together, they can support a stronger full-funnel advertising strategy.

Streaming TV Advertising

Best for businesses that want local or regional brand awareness, audience reach, video storytelling, and stronger visibility before customers enter the search stage.

  • Brand awareness
  • Audience and household targeting
  • Premium video visibility
  • Strong for demand creation

FAQ

Streaming TV advertising questions businesses ask before starting.

What is streaming TV advertising?

Streaming TV advertising places video ads within internet-connected television and streaming content environments. It can include connected TV, OTT, and related video placements.

Is streaming TV only for large brands?

No. Local and regional businesses can use streaming TV to build awareness in specific markets, support launches, and stay visible to defined audiences.

Do I need a video commercial?

Yes, you need video creative. WebAct can help with creative direction, messaging, landing page alignment, and how the campaign should connect to the rest of your advertising.

Can TV ads work with Google Ads and social ads?

Yes. A strong strategy can use TV ads for awareness, social ads for retargeting, and search ads to capture active demand.
