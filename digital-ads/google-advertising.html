FAQ

Google Ads questions businesses ask before getting started.

How much should my business spend on Google Ads?

Budget depends on your industry, location, competition, goals, and how quickly you want data. WebAct can review your market and recommend a starting budget that makes sense for testing and growth.

Can WebAct manage an existing Google Ads account?

Yes. WebAct can review existing campaigns, search terms, budgets, conversion tracking, landing pages, and account structure to identify what should be improved first.

Do I need landing pages?

Often, yes. Google Ads usually performs better when the landing page closely matches the ad, keyword, and visitor intent. WebAct can help improve existing pages or build new campaign landing pages.

How long does it take to see results?

Some campaigns produce early activity quickly, but meaningful optimization takes data. The first phase is usually focused on clean launch, tracking, search term review, budget control, and performance improvements.

Does WebAct help with conversion tracking?

Yes. Tracking is one of the most important parts of campaign management. WebAct can help track forms, calls, appointment actions, ecommerce activity, and other important conversion events.
