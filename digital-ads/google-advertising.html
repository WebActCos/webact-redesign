Google Ads FAQ

Questions businesses ask before starting Google Ads with WebAct.

How fast can Google Ads start producing leads?

Google Ads can start sending traffic quickly after launch, but performance improves as we collect search term, conversion, and lead quality data. The first month is usually focused on clean setup, tracking, and early optimization.

Do I need a new landing page?

Not always. If your current page is clear, fast, mobile-friendly, and aligned with the campaign, we can use it. If the page is vague or difficult to convert on, we recommend improving it before spending heavily.

Can WebAct manage local service campaigns?

Yes. We can manage search campaigns for local services and help connect them with Local Services Advertising, Google Business Profile work, and local landing pages.

What budget should I start with?

The right budget depends on your service area, competition, average customer value, and goals. We can recommend a starting range after reviewing your market and the keywords that matter most.

Can you track phone calls and form submissions?

Yes. We can help configure conversion tracking for calls, forms, buttons, ecommerce actions, and key website events so campaign decisions are based on real outcomes.

Do you manage ecommerce Google Ads?

Yes. We can help with Shopping campaigns, Performance Max, product feed checks, remarketing, and campaign structures focused on revenue.

Will you write the ad copy?

Yes. WebAct writes ads around your services, offers, locations, differentiators, and customer intent. We also test messaging over time as performance data comes in.

Can Google Ads work with SEO?

Yes. Google Ads can capture demand while SEO builds organic visibility. The two channels can share landing page insights, keyword data, and conversion learnings.
