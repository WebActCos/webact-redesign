Hosting & Support FAQ

Questions about website and ecommerce hosting.

How much does Business Website Hosting cost?

Simple and Professional Editor Business Hosting is $100 per month or $900 annually.

How much does Ecommerce Hosting cost?

Professional Editor Ecommerce Hosting is $200 per month or $1,800 annually.

What is included with Business Hosting?

Business Hosting includes the drag-and-drop editor, responsive website platform, Amazon cloud hosting, analytics, blog and personalization tools, a free 10-product store, SSL, automatic updates, automatic backups, unlimited bandwidth, unlimited file space, unlimited pages, and editor support.

What is included with Ecommerce Hosting?

Ecommerce Hosting includes all Business Hosting features plus a store with up to 2,000 products, product options, inventory tools, promotions, coupons, payment processing, shipping and tax tools, social connections, mobile order management, and access to third-party applications.

What is the difference between Simple Editor and Professional Editor?

Both support business websites and responsive editing. Professional Editor is intended for websites requiring more advanced editing, ecommerce, layouts, integrations, and website-management capabilities.

Is SSL included?

Yes. Business Hosting and Ecommerce Hosting include a free SSL certificate for secure HTTPS website browsing.

Are website backups included?

Yes. Automatic backups are included with both hosting plans.

Does Business Hosting include ecommerce?

Business Hosting includes a basic store for up to ten products. Businesses that need a larger catalog or advanced store-management tools should use Professional Editor Ecommerce Hosting.

Can I upgrade to Ecommerce Hosting later?

Yes. A business can begin with Business Hosting and move to Ecommerce Hosting as its product catalog and store-management needs grow.

Are custom website updates included?

Editor and hosting support are included. Custom design, development, content, product entry, SEO, marketing, and larger update projects may require separate pricing or a WebAct package.
