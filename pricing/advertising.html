Advertising Pricing FAQ

Questions about campaign pricing and advertising budgets.

How much does WebAct charge for an advertising campaign?

WebAct charges $300 per advertising campaign, plus the advertising budget paid directly to the selected platform.

Is the advertising budget included in the $300 fee?

No. The $300 fee covers campaign management. Your advertising budget is separate and is paid to Google, Microsoft, Meta, LinkedIn, YouTube, or another selected platform.

What counts as one campaign?

A campaign is a separately organized advertising initiative with its own platform, objective, targeting, budget, and settings. Multiple campaign types are priced separately.

Does every campaign type cost the same?

Yes. Google Search Ads, Microsoft Ads, display, remarketing, shopping, video, social media, and Local Services Ads are each $300 per campaign, plus your advertising budget.

How much should I spend on the advertising budget?

The appropriate budget depends on the platform, market, competition, location, service value, audience size, and campaign goals. WebAct can help recommend a practical starting budget.

Are landing pages included?

Campaign setup can connect to an existing website page. New custom landing-page design or development may require separate design pricing.

Can I run more than one campaign?

Yes. You can run multiple campaigns across different services, products, locations, audiences, or advertising platforms. Each campaign is priced at $300 plus its advertising budget.

Can advertising be combined with SEO and marketing?

Yes. WebAct can coordinate digital advertising with SEO, AEO, local listings, email marketing, website design, landing pages, and conversion improvements.
