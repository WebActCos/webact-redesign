Advertising Pricing FAQ

Questions about platforms, campaigns, and advertising budgets.

How much does WebAct charge for an advertising campaign?

WebAct charges $300 per campaign, plus the advertising budget paid to the selected platform.

Does every advertising platform have the same WebAct campaign fee?

Yes. Google, Local Services Ads, Microsoft, Amazon, social media, and streaming TV campaigns are each $300 per campaign plus the applicable advertising budget.

Is the advertising budget included in the $300 fee?

No. The $300 fee covers campaign management. Your advertising budget is separate and is paid to the selected advertising platform.

What counts as one campaign?

A campaign is a separately configured advertising initiative with its own platform, objective, targeting, settings, and budget.

Can WebAct manage Amazon Advertising?

Yes. Amazon campaign options can include Sponsored Products, Sponsored Brands, Sponsored Display, Amazon DSP, video, and streaming TV advertising.

Which social media platforms can WebAct advertise on?

Campaign options can include Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, Pinterest, Snapchat, Reddit, X, and Nextdoor, depending on platform availability and the campaign objective.

Which streaming TV platforms are included?

Streaming TV options may include Amazon Streaming TV, YouTube connected TV, Roku, Hulu and Disney inventory, Peacock, Paramount+, Tubi, and programmatic connected-TV inventory. Availability and minimum media budgets may vary.

Are landing pages and video production included?

Campaign setup can use existing approved assets and website pages. Custom landing pages, advanced graphic design, or video production may require separate design pricing.

Can I run several campaigns at the same time?

Yes. Each separately configured campaign is priced at $300 plus its advertising budget.
