Package Pricing FAQ

Questions about WebAct package pricing.

Are the packages monthly or annual?

WebAct packages are recurring monthly or annual plans. Annual plans include additional savings compared with paying monthly.

Do package prices include the advertising budget?

Managed advertising campaigns are included where listed, but the media budget paid to Google, Microsoft, Yelp, or another advertising platform is separate unless specifically stated in a proposal.

What is considered a website update request?

Website update requests generally include approved content, image, layout, link, service, product, or page edits within the scope of the selected package.

Can I upgrade packages later?

Yes. A business can begin with a smaller package and move to a larger package as its website, SEO, advertising, listings, and support needs increase.

Can I purchase services separately instead?

Yes. WebAct also offers a la carte pricing for website design, hosting, SEO, AEO, local listings, Google Business Profile services, email marketing, advertising, branding, and graphic design.

Which package is best for a new business?

Start Up is designed for smaller businesses with limited budgets. Grow adds a larger website and ongoing SEO support for businesses beginning to expand.

Which package includes advertising?

Boost includes one managed PPC campaign, Expand includes two, Flourish includes four, and Ultimate includes eight managed PPC campaigns plus a managed Yelp advertising campaign.

Which package includes reputation management?

Ultimate includes Local Listings Optimization with reputation management.

Does Ultimate include a dedicated account manager?

Yes. The Ultimate package includes a dedicated account manager along with the largest website, SEO, advertising, email, listings, and support allocation.
