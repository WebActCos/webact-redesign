Marketing Pricing FAQ

Questions about SEO, AEO, and marketing plans.

How much does SEO cost?

SEO pricing depends on competition, target services, locations, website condition, content needs, and whether the campaign is local or national. WebAct provides pricing after reviewing the scope.

What is the difference between Local SEO and National SEO?

Local SEO focuses on nearby customers, local searches, maps, service areas, and business profiles. National SEO targets broader markets, topics, services, and non-location-specific searches.

What is AEO?

Answer Engine Optimization organizes content so search engines and AI-powered answer systems can more clearly understand the business, services, expertise, and answers provided by the website.

Is AEO included with SEO?

AEO can be included as part of a broader SEO strategy or provided as a focused service. The right approach depends on the website, industry, content, and search goals.

How long does SEO take?

SEO is generally an ongoing process. Timing depends on competition, website authority, technical condition, content quality, location, industry, and how consistently improvements are made.

Are digital ads included in marketing pricing?

Digital advertising is priced separately unless it is included in a custom package. WebAct can coordinate SEO, marketing, ads, landing pages, and website improvements as one strategy.

Can WebAct manage multiple business locations?

Yes. WebAct can support multiple locations with location pages, Google Business Profile work, listings, local SEO, reviews, and market-specific content.

Can I choose only one marketing service?

Yes. A business can begin with one service, such as Local SEO, AEO, listings, or email marketing, and expand the plan later.
