Marketing Pricing FAQ

Questions about SEO, AEO, listings, profiles, and email pricing.

Are the SEO and AEO plans under contract?

No. Local SEO, National SEO, and AEO plans are offered monthly with no contract.

What is included in Local SEO Benchmark?

Local SEO Benchmark includes one new main keyword each month, four local towns or cities, four new articles, a website health checkup, local link building, a 50-keyword tracker, and ranking reports.

What is included in Local SEO Exclusive?

Local SEO Exclusive includes two new main keywords each month, five local towns or cities per keyword, ten new articles, a website checkup and audit, local link building, a 100-keyword tracker, and ranking reports.

What is included in National SEO Premium?

National SEO Premium includes 20 new main keywords each month, 20 new articles, a website checkup and audit, link building, a 200-keyword tracker, and ranking reports.

How is AEO different from traditional SEO?

Traditional SEO focuses on visibility in search-engine results. Answer Engine Optimization also structures content, services, questions, entities, and answers so AI-powered search and answer platforms can better understand the website.

What is included in AEO SEO Benchmark?

AEO SEO Benchmark includes five new AEO articles, five new SEO articles, a website health checkup, link building, a 50-keyword tracker, and ranking reports each month.

Are Local Listings plans priced per location?

Yes. DIY Listings, Managed Listings, and Managed Listings Plus are monthly plans priced separately for each business location.

What is included in Managed Listings Plus?

Managed Listings Plus includes location optimization, images and logo, products and services, verification of more than 100 featured listings, team bios and images, a featured message, monthly reports, and review monitoring and response.

Is Google My Business pricing monthly or one time?

Benchmark and Business are one-time plans priced per location. Premium is an ongoing monthly plan priced per location.

How much does email marketing cost?

Email campaigns start at $500 per campaign for up to 2,500 contacts. Larger contact lists, recurring campaigns, segmentation, and advanced design requirements receive custom pricing.

Can these services be combined?

Yes. WebAct can combine Local SEO, National SEO, AEO, local listings, Google Business Profile management, email marketing, website improvements, and advertising into a custom plan.

Ready to choose the right marketing plan?

Tell WebAct about your website, locations, target services, current visibility, and advertising goals. We can recommend the right combination of SEO, AEO, listings, profiles, PPC, and ongoing marketing support.
